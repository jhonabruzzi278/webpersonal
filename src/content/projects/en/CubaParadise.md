Tourism web platform specialized in authentic Cuban experiences. Offers tours through Havana and Viñales, accommodation in private houses, car rentals and salsa classes. Developed with Astro, Node.js and TailwindCSS, it connects travelers with expert local guides to discover true Cuban culture in a personalized and authentic way.
